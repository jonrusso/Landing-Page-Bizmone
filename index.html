Who we are?

Bizmone was founded by two lifelong surfers who were passionate about creating high-quality, sustainable products for the surfing community.

Our team is made up of passionate and experienced surfers, designers, and marketers who are dedicated to bringing the best possible products and services to our customers.

We believe that surfing and a connection to the ocean are essential for a healthy and balanced lifestyle. That's why we're committed to creating products that help people connect with the ocean and live their best lives.

Our concept

Bizmone was inspired by the idea of combining the laid-back, free-spirited lifestyle of surfing with a modern and sustainable approach to fashion and design.

Our products are made from eco-friendly materials and are designed to be both stylish and functional, so you can feelling good while enjoying the surf and the ocean.

We believe that surfing is more than just a sport - it's a way of life, and we want to help people embrace that lifestyle in everything they do.
